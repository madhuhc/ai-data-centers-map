About This Map

This interactive map tracks all major AI data center construction projects currently underway or recently started worldwide. It covers facilities being built by OpenAI, Meta, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, xAI, Anthropic, and others.

Data includes investment amounts, power capacity, GPU deployments, job creation, and construction status. Each marker is sized by power capacity and colored by company.

The AI infrastructure buildout represents the largest single-purpose construction effort in modern history, with over $700 billion in committed investment and dozens of gigawatts of power capacity under development.

Data compiled from public sources as of March 2026. Click any marker for detailed information and source links.
